\subsection{Subsection Example --- Acronyms} \label{sec:acronym}

This section shows how to use the acronyms \ac{AI} and \ac{ML}.  
Later we can simply refer to \ac{AI} or \ac{ML} without expansion.

In modern technology, \ac{NLP} plays a crucial role in virtual assistants 
and chatbots.  
We also rely on \ac{GPU} hardware for deep learning models.

\subsubsection{Special use in titles as \acs*{FEUP}}

Acronyms can also be made plural: \acp{GPU} are widely used in 
high-performance computing.  
Possessive forms are supported too: \ac{AI}'s impact on society is 
profound at \ac{FEUP}.
